7 Rules
#Rule
1Eat 40–50g protein at every meal — anabolic resistance after 40 means every meal must trigger muscle protein synthesis
230–40g carbs 30–45 min before HIIT — glycogen is your fuel, never train on empty
340–50g protein + 50g carbs within 45 min post-training — highest MPS window of the day
4Keep dietary fat at 30–35% of total calories — testosterone is synthesized from cholesterol, never drop fat below 65g/day
5Drink 3–4 liters of water daily — HIIT dehydration reduces power output by up to 20%
6Maximum 1–2 drinks per week — alcohol suppresses testosterone for 24–72 hours
7Never drop below 1,800 calories/day at this volume — severe restriction burns muscle not fat
